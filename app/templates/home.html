ReefGauge is a learning tool that blends data from the scientific community and the diving community to help divers, researchers, and ocean advocates better understand reef health. Coral reefs are stabilized by a balanced relationship between coral, fish, and benthos. Start exploring to learn about the different reef health indicators and to become a more empowered ambassador for coral reefs.
