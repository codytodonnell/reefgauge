About

ReefGauge is an early-stage experimental project out of the Georgia Institute of Technology. The tool's goal is make environmental data about the ocean a more engaging resource for divers and ocean enthusiasts. You're seeing the first version of the tool which is exclusively focused on data in and around the Caribbean. The hope is that this project will continue to grow to include data about reefs all over the world.
